ECFS   399  ISSUES & TRENDS(I&S)
       21268 A  3       Th     430-650P   MLR  320      RYAN,JOEL                  Open     19/  40                      
                        THIS CLASS WILL EXPLORE THE DIRECT                          
                        IMPACT OF THE COVID-19 PANDEMIC AND                         
                        ENSUING ECONOMIC CRISIS ON LOW                              
                        INCOME CHILDREN AND FAMILIES.                               
                        WE WILL FOCUS ON HOW POVERTY                                
                        AND RACE DISPARITIES                                        
                        IMPACT THE HEALTH OF MARGINALIZED                           
                        POPULATIONS, THE DISTRIBUTION OF                            
                        VACCINES, THE EFFECTS OF REMOTE                             
                        SCHOOLING AND LEARNING LOSS, AND                            
                        WHETHER IT MAKES SENSE TO HAVE                              
                        STUDENTS RETURN TO IN PERSON                                
                        CLASSROOMS. WE WILL ALSO LOOK AT                            
                        THE CHALLENGES OF FINDING                                   
                        AFFORDABLE QUALITY CHILD CARE                               
                        AS WELL AS HUNGER AND HOUSING                               
                        INSECURITY ISSUES THAT ARE                                  
                        A DIRECT RESULT OF COVID-19.                                
                        FINALLY, THIS CLASS WILL EXAMINE                            
                        THE EFFORTS OF PRIVATE PHILANTHROPY                         
                        AND THE RESPONSE OF THE STATE AND                           
                        FEDERAL GOVERNMENT TO THIS CRISIS.                          
                        DURING CLASS, WE WILL                                       
                        HAVE IN-DEPTH CONVERSATIONS AND                             
                        HEAR PRESENTATIONS FROM LEADING                             
                        EXPERTS FROM LOW-INCOME FAMILIES                            
                        FROM ACROSS WASHINGTON STATE.
