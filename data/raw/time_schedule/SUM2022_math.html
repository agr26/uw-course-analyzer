OVERLOAD POLICY - STUDENTS SHOULD NOT CONTACT THE INSTRUCTORS. OVERLOADS WILL BE AVAILABLE ON A LIMITED BASIS. PLEASE CONTACT THE MATH ADVISING OFFICE, 543-6830, C-36 PADELFORD, FOR MORE INFO. TO REGISTER FOR MATH 111, OR 120 STUDENTS WILL NEED A PLACEMENT TEST. TO REGISTER FOR MATH 124, MOST STUDENTS WILL NEED EITHER A PLACEMENT TEST OR PREVIOUS COMPLETION OF MATH 120 (FORMERLY MATH 105) WITH A GRADE OF 2.5 OR HIGHER. STUDENTS CURRENTLY ENROLLED IN MATH 120 MAY REGISTER FOR MATH 124, BUT WILL BE DISENROLLED BY THE FIRST DAY OF CLASS IF THEY DO NOT COMPLETE MATH 120 WITH A GRADE OF 2.5 OR HIGHER. THE REQUIREMENTS ARE DESCRIBED IN THE SHEET "PLACEMENT REQUIREMENTS FOR MATH 107,111,120 OR 124" WHICH IS AVAILABLE AT UNDERGRADUATE ADVISING, 171 MARY GATES HALL OR MATH ADVISING, PDL C036.
