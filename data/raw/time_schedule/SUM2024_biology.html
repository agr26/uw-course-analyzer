                        THROUGH CRITICALLY READING                                  
                        SCIENTIFIC LITERATURE, ANALYZING

                        INTERPRETING PUBLISHED DATA.                                
       10481 B  2       MW     220-430    LSB  201      Martin-Morris She. They,Li Open     14/  24                      
                        A-term                                                      
                        UNVEILING THE MACHINERY OF THE CELL                         
                        PROTEIN STRUCTURES AND DYNAMICS.                            
                        CELL FUNCTION THROUGH THE                                   
                        INTRICATELY                                                 
                        ORCHESTRATED INTERACTIONS OF                                
                        BIOMOLECULAR STRUCTURES, INCLUDING                          
                        NUCLEIC ACIDS, PROTEINS, AND LIPIDS                         
                        STRUCTURAL BIOLOGY CAN REVEAL THE                           
                        GEOMETRY OF THESE BIOMOLECULES WITH                         
                        ATOMIC PRECISION, ENABLING THE                              
                        DISCOVERY OF THEIR STRUCTURE-                               
                        FUNCTION RELATIONSHIPS. THE NEXT                            
                        FRONTIER IN STRUCTURAL BIOLOGY IS                           
                        TO STUDY THE STRUCTURAL FUNCTIONS                           
                        OF BIOMOLECULES. IN THIS COURSE                             
                        STUDENTS WILL GAIN HISTORICAL                               
                        CONTEXT FOR STRUCTURAL BIOLOGY                              
                        METHODS SUCH AS SPECTROSCOPY, X-RAY                         
                        DIFFRACTION, AND COMPUTATIONAL                              
                        METHODS. WE WILL DISCUSS THE NEWEST                         
                        DEVELOPMENTS BY READING RECENT                              
                        PRIMARY LITERATURE REVEALING NEW                            
                        INSIGHTS INTO THE FUNCTION OF ION                           
                        CHANNELS, DNA REPAIR, AND MUSCLE                            
                        MOLECULAR MOTORS. THESE DISCOVERIES                         
                        WILL BE SET AGAINST A HISTORICAL                            
                        BACKDROP, ENCOURAGING A HOLISTIC                            
                        DISCUSSION OF THIS EMERGING FIELD'S                         
                        POTENTIAL AND LIMITATIONS.
